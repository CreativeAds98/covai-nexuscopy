Finance & Accounting

At CovAI Nexus, we help organizations transform traditional finance functions into intelligent, data-driven operations that ensure accuracy, compliance, and strategic agility.

Finance & Accounting Services

Our finance services go beyond transactional processing — we embed automation, real-time reporting, and compliance-by-design to help enterprises scale operations without scaling cost.

Our Capabilities Include:
  • ● Accounts Payable & Receivable Automation: Streamline invoicing, payment tracking, and vendor/customer interactions using automation and smart validations.
  • ● General Ledger & Closing Support: Maintain audit-ready books, ensure reconciliation accuracy, and accelerate month-end closings with SLA-bound processes.
  • ● Expense Management & Reimbursements: Digitally track claims, enforce policy adherence, and automate approval workflows to reduce leakages and cycle time.
  • ● Bank Reconciliations: Leverage automation bots to reconcile bank statements with real-time ERP feeds.
  • ● Intercompany Transactions & Tax Handling: Support for transfer pricing, intercompany balancing, and GST/TDS management (India-specific, customizable globally).
Why Choose Us?
  • ● Digital First: RPA + Policy-Aware GenAI Copilots for validations and insights
  • ● Transparent Dashboards: Real-time SLA, accuracy, and aging reports
  • ● Continuous Improvement: Monthly retrospectives + Kaizen workshops
  • ● Audit Ready: Traceable, version-controlled financial documentation
